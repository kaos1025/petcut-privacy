# PetCut Data Deletion Request

**Last Updated: May 06, 2026**

---

## Overview

PetCut operates on a **server-less architecture**. All your personal data — including pet profiles, analysis history, and label images — is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not retain any user data on our own servers.

---

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1: Uninstall the App (Recommended)

Uninstalling PetCut from your device will **immediately and permanently delete all of your data**, including:

- Pet profile information (name, species, breed, weight, age)
- Analysis history (scan results, combo reports)
- Cached label images
- Locally stored in-app purchase records

**Steps (Android):**

1. Long-press the PetCut app icon on your device's home screen
2. Tap "Uninstall" or drag the app to the "Uninstall" area
3. Confirm uninstall

All locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

### Option 2: Email Request

If you have specific data deletion requests or any privacy concerns, please contact us:

📧 **Email:** [support@supplecut.com](mailto:support@supplecut.com)

We will respond within 7 business days.

---

## What Data Is Retained After Uninstall?

| Location | Data Retained |
|---|---|
| **Your device** | None — all data deleted on uninstall |
| **Our servers** | None — PetCut does not operate any servers |
| **Third-party AI services** | Inputs sent for processing are not permanently stored after analysis is complete, in accordance with each provider's data retention policies |

For third-party AI service policies, see:

- [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
