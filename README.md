# PetCut Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 06, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to PetCut ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

> 💡 **Summary:** We collect minimal data necessary to provide our pet food and supplement combo analysis service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information you provide directly:**

- **Pet Food and Supplement Images:** Photos of pet food and supplement labels you upload for combo analysis
- **Pet Profile Information:** Pet name, species (dog or cat), breed, weight, and age — used solely for weight-based toxicity analysis and stored locally on your device
- **Analysis History:** Results of your combo analyses (stored locally on your device)
- **Purchase Information:** If you make in-app purchases (paid detailed reports), payment is processed by Google Play; we do not store payment details

**Information collected automatically:**

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features used, analysis frequency
- **Crash Reports:** Technical data to improve app stability

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and improve our pet food and supplement combo analysis service
- Process your label images using AI technology to identify ingredients and calculate combined nutrient intake
- Generate weight-based toxicity analysis tailored to your pet's species, breed, and body weight
- Generate paid 5-section detailed reports (Pet Risk Profile, Combo Load Report, Mechanism Alerts, Observable Warning Signs, Action Plan)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

PetCut operates on a **server-less architecture**. Your pet profile information and combo analysis history are stored locally on your device only — we do not retain any user data on our own servers.

Images and analysis input data sent for processing are transmitted to Google's Gemini AI (free analysis tier) and Anthropic's Claude AI (paid detailed reports tier), and are not permanently stored on those services' servers after analysis is complete, in accordance with each provider's data retention policies.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including HTTPS encryption in transit, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Gemini AI:** For pet food and supplement label image analysis (free tier)
- **Anthropic Claude AI:** For generating paid detailed nutrition reports
- **Google Play Billing:** For in-app purchases

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

---

## 6. Your Rights (CCPA Compliance)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- **Know:** Request information about the personal data we collect
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
- **Non-Discrimination:** Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

Since PetCut stores all pet profile and analysis data locally on your device only, you can delete all personal data by uninstalling the app. No personal data is retained on our servers after analysis is complete.

To exercise these rights or for any privacy inquiries, contact us at [support@supplecut.com](mailto:support@supplecut.com).

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
